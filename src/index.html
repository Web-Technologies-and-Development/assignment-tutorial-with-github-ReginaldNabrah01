At Nabrah's Health Care Centre, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive health education and services to our community. Our mission is to promote healthy lifestyles and prevent health issues through awareness and education.

Teenage Pregnancy

Teenage pregnancy occurs when a girl under the age of 20 becomes pregnant. It is a global issue that affects education, health, and future opportunities for young people.

Causes of Teenage Pregnancy

  • Lack of proper sex education
  • Peer pressure and influence from friends
  • Poverty and limited opportunities
  • Broken homes and lack of parental guidance
  • Media influence and exposure to sexual content

Effects of Teenage Pregnancy

  • Dropout from school and limited career opportunities
  • Health risks for both mother and baby
  • Emotional and psychological stress
  • Financial difficulties
  • Increased risk of social stigma and discrimination

Solutions to Teenage Pregnancy

  • Comprehensive sex education in schools and communities
  • Parental guidance and open communication
  • Access to contraception and healthcare services
  • Empowerment programs for young girls
  • Encouraging abstinence and responsible behavior
